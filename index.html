🌸 our story

Mina is a tiny macaron café nestled in the heart of Bebek, Istanbul. Every morning we bake small batches using local ingredients — coconut flour, almond, and a touch of rose.

We believe in real food, real flavors, and real joy. Our macarons are gluten-free, and we offer vegan options with coconut sugar and oat milk.
